About Us

Thank you for visiting the official website for Sunrise Dental Clinic.

Our clinic was founded in 2019 by Dr. Shehab AlaaElDin with our main branch in Tagamoa (5th settlement), New Cairo. We provide all kinds of dental treatments for the whole family at affordable prices.

All our team have completed or are doing post-graduate UK-based specialist training from the Royal College of Surgeons of England or Edinburgh. We proudly bring British standards of dentistry to Egypt.
